Marinated Feta

*** Recipe and image by Serious Keto ***

Ingredients

  • rind from one medium lemon, cut into thin ribbons
  • 1 serrano pepper, thinly sliced
  • 2 TB roughly chopped flat leaf parsley
  • 1.5 TB zahtar (or za’atar)
  • 1⁄4 tsp kosher salt
  • 12-15 grinds black pepper
  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil (240mL)
  • 1 pound block feta in brine (450g)

Steps

  1. Whisk together all ingredients except feta in a medium bowl or Pyrex measuring cup.
  2. Cut feta into cubes, roughly 3/4 inch or 2 cm in size and place in a glass baking dish.
  3. Pour marinade over feta, cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 2-3 days.
  4. Serve with crackers, olives or as part of a charcuterie tray.
